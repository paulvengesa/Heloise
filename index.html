OUR MENU

✻

Guided by the old Polish kitchen tales and tricks we proud
ourselves to provide the revived dishes of our ancestors in
the new outlook. Polish pierogies, tripe soup or even
karmonadel cutlet will you find alongside pumpkin or salmon
variations in our a la carte menu.

As we understand that the good menu creation is a process
that takes some time we would like to ask you for your kind
or even not so... opinion to make our services the best of its
kind.
